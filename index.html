About

Hi Parents!

My name is Alek Kelly. I’m a former SVSD student and North Bend resident of 14 years. I started as a math tutor at Mathnasium, where I worked with students from kindergarten through 12th grade. I’ve been a private tutor since 2020, and will be making it my focus this year.

To accomplish this, I am welcoming new students!

Math tutoring is my primary service, but my goal is to help your student be successful in everything. This involves addressing foundational learning gaps while building up secondary skills. Your student should feel empowered, not overwhelmed, when tackling new challenges.

I hold a Bachelor of Science (BASc) in Computing and Software Development from LW Tech. My work as an IT Lab Technician contracted to Microsoft equipped me with excellent problem-solving and analytical skills. I currently work at Trader Joe’s in Issaquah, where I organize and feature new products in the store. Outside of work, I’m an active member in the local running community.

Before we begin, I’d love to connect with you to understand your goals and your student’s needs. Let’s schedule a time to chat!
